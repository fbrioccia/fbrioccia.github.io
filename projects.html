Dungeon Sickness

Dungeon Sickness is a 2D top-view action game.

Players find themselves in a dungeon with no information on why they are there and fight their way to the exit solving the mistery.

Dungeon Sickness features both single and multiplayer campaigns with hundreds of items, monsters and random-generated levels.
